Welcome to The Undermine Journal ...your source for Auction House statistics and data in World of Warcraft.

Saturday, October 11, 2014
We continue to tweak and update things here as time permits. Visit our official forum for more information and feedback about recent additions, and answers to many questions about what features are coming up.

The 6.0 patch is coming up in a few days on Tuesday. The latest blue post suggests that Blizzard's new, 2-month-old API may not be functional in the time between 6.0 and the expansion's release in mid-November. This is a huge setback to all websites that use Blizzard's API, including the many sites that use their auction house data. We anticipate that we will have no auction data for any realm after Tuesday.

After the community causes the appropriate amount of ruckus, at some point we'll get auction data back. We intend to clear all our history data when data collection resumes since the factions will have joined auction houses and the faction-specific history will be irrelevant. Plus, we will be able to clear out any data issues caused during the beta phase of TUJ's rerelease.

We'll continue to keep you updated as the patches are applied and APIs are eventually updated. Hopefully we won't be without data for too long.

We encourage you to use our official forum for your comments and suggestions, so we can cut down on answering the same question multiple times.
